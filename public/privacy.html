Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from your Google account. When you sign in with a password, we store the name and role you provide.

Your information is used solely to:

  • Associate assignments, recordings, and feedback with the correct users

Data Storage

Your data is stored on our server and is not shared with third parties. Audio recordings you upload are stored securely and are only accessible to you and your assigned teacher.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting the app administrator.
